Chicken Chowmein

Chicken Chowmein can be a little stressful to make at times but it tastes delicous. It also makes for great leftovers

A delicious meal for an energetic occasion!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Onion
  • Chowmein Noodles
  • Black Bean Sauce

Steps

  1. Chop Onion Chicken and Bean Sprouts
  2. Cook Chicken in wok at high heat until mostly cooked
  3. Add Black Bean Sauce
  4. Add Noodle Bean Sprout and Onions
  5. Cover and reduce heat for 2-3 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
