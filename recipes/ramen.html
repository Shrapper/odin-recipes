Ramen

Ramen is great meal for when you're not feeling motivated but still need to satisfy that whole hunger things

A lazy meal for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Seasoning Package

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Ramen
  3. Cook for 3-5 minutes
  4. Add seasoning
  5. Enjoy!
