Fajitas

Chicken Fajitas are a childhood favorite for me. This recipe is pretty good but nothing can compare to mom's!

A tasty treat for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Tortilla Wraps

Steps

  1. Chop Chicken Peppers and Onions
  2. Fry Chicken on medium to high heat until chicken cooked
  3. Add Peppers and Onions about 2-3 minutes before finished cooking
  4. Grate Cheese on Tortillas
  5. Add Salsa
  6. Put cooked ingredients on Tortillas
  7. Enjoy!
